# Farm Experience Day

Get your hands dirty and experience authentic farm life! Our Farm Experience Day lets you participate in daily activities and learn about sustainable agriculture.

## A Day on the Farm

- **Duration**: Full day (9am - 5pm)
- **Includes**: Lunch and refreshments
- **Suitable for**: All ages

## Activities Include

### Morning
- Feeding the animals (chickens, goats, and sheep)
- Collecting fresh eggs
- Milking goats (seasonal)
- Garden work and harvesting

### Afternoon
- Olive grove maintenance
- Traditional tool demonstrations
- Seasonal activities (pruning, planting, harvesting)
- Animal care and husbandry basics

## What You'll Take Home

- A basket of fresh farm produce
- Recipes and tips for sustainable living
- Unforgettable memories and new skills
- A deeper connection to the land

This experience is perfect for families, couples, or anyone wanting to reconnect with nature and traditional farming practices.
