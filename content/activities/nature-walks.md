# Nature Walks at Moinhos Do Dao

Join us for guided nature walks through the stunning landscapes surrounding our eco-farm. Our trails wind through ancient forests, along crystal-clear streams, and past traditional vineyards.

## What to Expect

- **Duration**: 2-3 hours
- **Difficulty**: Easy to moderate
- **Group size**: Up to 10 people

## Highlights

- Learn about native plants and their traditional uses
- Spot local wildlife including birds, deer, and wild boar
- Visit scenic viewpoints overlooking the Dao valley
- Discover hidden waterfalls and natural springs

## What to Bring

- Comfortable walking shoes
- Water bottle
- Sun protection
- Camera

We provide walking sticks and binoculars upon request.
