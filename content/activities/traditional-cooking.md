# Traditional Portuguese Cooking

Immerse yourself in the flavors of Portugal with our hands-on cooking workshop. Using fresh ingredients from our farm and garden, you'll learn to prepare authentic regional dishes.

## The Experience

- **Duration**: 4 hours
- **Includes**: All ingredients, recipes, and a shared meal
- **Group size**: 4-8 people

## What You'll Learn

- Traditional bread making in our wood-fired oven
- Classic Portuguese soups and stews
- Seasonal vegetable dishes from our garden
- Local desserts and sweet treats

## Farm-to-Table

Everything we cook comes from our farm or local producers:
- Fresh vegetables from our organic garden
- Eggs from our free-range chickens
- Olive oil from our grove
- Wine from neighboring vineyards

At the end of the workshop, we sit down together to enjoy the meal you've prepared.
