# Ecological Gardening Workshop

Date: 2014-11-18 14:39:17

Ecological Gardening Workshop:  growing food the Permaculture way
May 4th - 11th, 2014
Led by Rob van Deursen - Stichting Saiwala
Moinhos do Dão, Mangualde, Portugal

Enjoyment and learning are a perfect pair!  Come and experience it during this Ecological Gardening & Permaculture workshop, taking place at a “piece of paradise" in Central Portugal.

During this workshop we will observe, design, plan, improve and expand a kitchen garden on an
eco-quinta in the Dão valley of Central Portugal. We will use permaculture ethics, strategy and design framework to enhance a food production system that fits the landscape, suits the people and enhances ecosystems. The workshop covers everything from survey to implementation of a 250m2 Horta \(kitchen garden\) in a mix of theory and practical activities. We’ll be working on all levels—from the garden layout up through the details of planning and planting.

Subjects in this workshop \(roughly per day\):

**Beginning: Introductions and Survey **\(5th of May\)
The Permaculture approach, observation of the land, base-map creation.
**Foundation: Soil**
Natural soil, life in the soil, soil sampling, diversity, composting, soil improvement.
**Design**
Design strategies & patterns, selection and sizing of garden elements. Draft garden layout. \(free afternoon\)
**Water & Landscaping**
Natural water flows, irrigation. Final garden layout.
**Annuals & Perennials**
Crop rotation, polycultures, planning, planting.
**Wrap-Up **\(10th of May\)
Review of design and implementation plan, improvements. \(free afternoon\)

This 6-day workshop intensive is for anyone who is interested in organic gardening in harmony with nature. Some basic knowledge, of one or more of the following fields; horticulture, biology, ecology, Permaculture design and nature preservation, is required. The workshop will be given in English and some Nederlands \(Dutch\).

Costs for a week: € 350, includes 7 nights lodging from the 4th till the 11th of May, course materials and small textbook, breakfast, lunch and dinner, with shared accommodation. \(Participants will stay in the rustic guest quarters of Moinhos do Dão.\) Single day participation:  € 15 for workshop and € 15 for lunch and dinner. \(Single night stay is optional\)

\* Moinhos do Dão, is a former miller’s farm of 2 acres located on the banks of the Dão River. Moinhos do Dão welcomes guests, residents, volunteers and day visitors to observe and experience off-the-grid living in harmony with nature.
