# Lodging for the Residency Program

Date: 2021-01-27 11:28:59

Comfortable and private self-catering accommodations for the lodging for

our guests in the residency program and additional vacationing guests.


A truly unique 2 person accommodation located above the old mill channels. The living room \(3 x 4.5 mtr\) has large opening windows and looks out on the water channel that flows under the building. The adjacent former mill room \(3.5 x 4 mtr\) has a double bed and kitchenette. A small but sweet and special place to stay and dream away by the sound of the water flow.

**Facilities include:**

Kitchen, Double bed, Small couch for 2, Ceiling lamp in each room, Toilet en suite, Wifi.

**Vacation Rental Prices:**

April, May, September, October: **€ 300** per week

June, July, August: **€ 380** per week

Minimum stay is 2 nights and price per night is 1/6 of week price.

Prices include cleaning and linen.

Check in is between 15h and 20h & Check out is before 11h



This cabin is privately located on one of the higher terraces of the terrain and has a nice view of the quinta and the surrounding hills.
