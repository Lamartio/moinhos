# Holidays at Moinhos do Dão

Date: 2024-06-18 14:54:04


Our holiday rental Casa Boa Vista has everything you need for a comfortable getaway in nature. It is bright and cozy and has all the facilities and privacy for an independent stay at this quinta.

Boa Vista has an unique open kitchen in the living room, an adjacent bedroom and ‘en-suite’ bathroom. The front door opens to a small porch with a view on the heart of the quinta and the backdoor leads to private backyard surrounded by nature with a bench and outdoor table with chairs. The apartment can be used for couples and families with young children. For families with older children \(8/10+\) we suggest placing a tent on a field near the apartment. Tent incl. beds can be rented here.


**Facilities include:**

Bathroom with warm shower, toilet and sink

Kitchen incl. fridge

Bedroom with double bed

Fold out couch

Wood heater

Wifi

**Prices:**

April, May, September, October: **€ 60 per night & € 360 per week.**

June, July, August: **€** 75 **per night & € 525** **per week.**
Minimum stay is 2 nights.

Prices include cleaning and linen.

Check in is between 15h and 20h & Check out is before 11h


\~ Feel at home at Moinhos do Dão. \~

As a guest at Moinhos do Dão you have access to most parts of the terrain. You can explore the vegetable garden, wild island in the middle of the Dão river and relax in one of the many beautiful spots on the Dão river. We are also open to involving guests with working on general activities such as gardening, maintenance of the terrain and facilities, forestry work, cleaning and meal preparation.
